What is PregCare-Gh?

PregCare-Gh is an eHealth portal that provides healthcare information to support pregnant women. PregCare-Gh has range of features that are tailored to meet specific needs of pregnant women.

What to know about Pregnancy?

Pregnancy is a crucial period in a woman's life where she carries a developing fetus in her uterus. It is a complex biological process that involves various changes in the woman's body to support the growth and development of the fetus.
